ShiftCare

A 2D nurse triage simulator. Assess patients, deliver care, and complete documentation every shift.

How to Play

Move with WASD or Arrow Keys. Walk into a patient to open the interaction menu.

Follow the care workflow: Vitals → Nursing Diagnosis → Medication → Care → SBAR* → Documentation → Exit

*SBAR escalation required for Red and Yellow patients only

Red = Critical · Yellow = Urgent · Green = Low Priority

Select Shift

Complete shifts to unlock harder missions.

Patient Interaction

Select an action to begin care.

Vital Signs Assessment

Select each vital to record. Required vitals are marked. Collect equipment from the ward first.

💊

Administer Medication

Prepare and administer the prescribed medication to the patient.



Patient Care & Support

Complete the required care obligations for this patient before handover.
